Cornbread

Mixed Opinion Bread

Cornbread is a type of bread which is made diffrently depending on where you are from. It can either be sweet or savory, it can be crumbly or more cake-like, and it can be eaten be itself or with a meal.

I do like cornbread no matter which way it is made. However, I would not go out of my way seeking it out.
