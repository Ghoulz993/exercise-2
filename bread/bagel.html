Bagel

Breakfast Delight

Bagels are a type of bread which have a hole in the middle. They can be eaten toasted or fresh, and can be topped with different spreads or eaten as a sandwich.

I enjoy eating bagels. I sometimes eat them for breakfast, but I usually have them as part of my lunch.
